Primary Colour Palette
Mipa Blue:

This captivating shade of Mipa Blue mirrors the expanse of a clear, azure sky stretching over an open horizon. Just as the sky holds the potential for growth and exploration, this blue embodies our commitment to progress and leadership. It symbolizes trust, stability, and depth, resonating with our journey of guiding and shaping industries with precision.

Pure White:

The hue of Pure White is a canvas of clarity and simplicity. Like a clean slate, it embraces the light and embodies our value of simplicity. This white hue welcomes all to explore the intricate layers of our brand, free from complexities. It's an invitation to engage with our offerings with an open mind, where the essence of our brand shines brightly.

Mystic Grey:

Our Mystic Grey serves as the sturdy foundation upon which our brand thrives. Like the solid ground beneath us, this neutral grey offers a stable backdrop, reflecting our value of control. It stands as a steadfast anchor that harmonizes our visual identity, ensuring that every element finds its place with elegance and sophistication.

Brand Colours

All brand colors carry equal significance and must consistently appear in their full, vibrant hue. Modifying shades through darkening, lightening, or incorporating transparency overlays is not permissible.



Avoid altering the intensity of the brand colours through darkening or lightening.
