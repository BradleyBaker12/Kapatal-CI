Embodying the Essence: Why Roboto Condensed

Amongst the array of fonts, Roboto Condensed stands as the ideal embodiment of our brand's identity. This font resonates with our values of Control, Growth, Leadership, Simplicity, and German Standards, bringing forth a harmonious fusion of form and function.
Roboto Condensed

Utilize Roboto Condensed across all customer communications, encompassing both print and online platforms.

Utilize Roboto Condensed Bold and Medium variations for headings, and employ Regular and Light weights for body text. Select the appropriate font weight based on the target market or as necessitated by business requirements.

Application

Typography can be utilized in either black or white, with the inclusion of grey scales allowed in digital contexts for functional considerations.

There's flexibility in choosing type sizes, line spacing, and the spacing between text hierarchies. For headlines and sublines, Gilroy Heavy and Bold is employed.


Typography always appears

- in ragged type,
- preferably flush left or flush right,
- and in mixed case.
