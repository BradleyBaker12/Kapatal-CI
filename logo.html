Logo Spacing Guidelines.

Unified Presence

Minimum Size Guidelines for the Emblem.

Delving into the realm of 'Minimum Size Guidelines,' we illuminate the dimensions that ensure our emblem's impact and clarity remain unwavering.

The minimum height for digital applications is set at 100 pixels, while for analogue applications, it measures 15 mm in positive space and 20 mm in negative space.
